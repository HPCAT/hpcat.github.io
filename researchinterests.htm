Research Interests

  • Computer Architecture, Energy-efficient Chip Multiprocessors, Parallel Processing, Fault-tolerant
    Multiprocessors, Reconfigurable and self-healing computing systems.

  • Reliable and fault-tolerant Network-on-Chips (NoCs) for multi-core architectures and embedded
    systems.

  • Nano-photonics for Network-on-Chips (NoCs)

  • Heterogeneous Interconnect technologies (photonics, 3-D stacking, RF) for scalable Network-on-Chips
    (NoCs)

  • Optical interconnects

  • Dynamic bandwidth allocation and dynamic power management techniques for optical interconnects

  • Interconnection networks for parallel computing systems
